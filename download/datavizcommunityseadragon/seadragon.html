Visualizing the structure of the dataviz commumity on Twitter

  • Data was kindly provided by Jeff Clark (@JeffClark) and is available here: http://dl.dropbox.com/u/18717701/DataVisField1.zip
  • Initial data: a list of triples: {datavizer A, datavizer B mentioned in the tweets of A,frequency of these mentions}
  • I applied a similarity measure: "connect A to B only if they frequently refer to the same other datavizers C, D, E, ... in their tweets.
  • The result is imported in Gephi and the usual treatment (community detection) is applied.
  • Note the size of the node refers to a centrality measure unrelated to the similarity exercise.
    It is simply: "how many times a datavizer is mentioned by other accounts"
