Explore the graph by left-clicking and dragging around

 

The color of the nodes represent macro - disciplines (18 in total).
The size of the nodes represents the proportion of papers of neuroeconomics published in the journals of this particular field.
For example, for a graph on neuroeconomics: a large node size for "neurosciences" means that in 1999-2009, neuroeconomics was predominantly published in journals of neuroscience.
